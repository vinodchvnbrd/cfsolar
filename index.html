Our Story

Pioneering Solar Excellence in India

Founded in 2010, SolaireTek began as a small solar installation company in Mumbai with a vision to make clean energy accessible to every Indian household and business. Over the years, we have grown into a full-fledged solar EPC company with a pan-India presence.

Today, SolaireTek stands as a testament to Indian engineering excellence, having successfully commissioned over 500 MW of solar capacity across residential, commercial, and utility-scale projects. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep understanding of India's renewable energy landscape.

As an authorized vendor under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijali Yojana and various state solar schemes, we have helped thousands of families embrace solar energy while enjoying government subsidies and incentives.

End-to-End Solutions

Solar EPC Services

Complete engineering, procurement, and construction services for solar power projects of all scales.

Utility Scale Solar EPC

Turn-key solutions for large-scale solar power plants from 1 MW to 100+ MW including land assessment, grid studies, design engineering, procurement, construction, testing, and commissioning with performance guarantees.

Commercial Solar Solutions

Customized rooftop and ground-mounted solar systems for offices, factories, warehouses, hospitals, hotels, and educational institutions with CAPEX, OPEX, and PPA models available.

Industrial Solar Plants

High-capacity solar installations for manufacturing units and industrial estates with load management, peak demand optimization, and HT/LT integration capabilities.

Residential Solar Systems

Complete home solar solutions including on-grid, off-grid, and hybrid systems under PM Surya Ghar Yojana with subsidy assistance, financing options, and net metering support.

Floating Solar

Innovative floating solar installations on water bodies, reservoirs, and ponds that reduce land usage, minimize evaporation, and provide higher efficiency due to natural cooling.

PM-KUSUM Solar

Complete solutions under PM-KUSUM scheme including Component-A (decentralized ground-mounted plants), Component-B (standalone solar pumps), and Component-C (grid-connected pump solarization).
